In Loving Memory of Family and Friends

A Living Record of the Lives of Our Loved Ones

Reich Memorials provides private memorials for friends and family. It serves as a depository for genealogical records, historical accounts, and memories of those who have passed, in written form, photos, and life accounts according to family members and friends.

While memorials here are no secret, links to them from the main page are not provided. Instead, links are personally provided to friends and family. Memorials can be shared on social media by friends and family members that contains an image, title, and description in a link preview for each memorial.

Every effort is made to be accurate in the written account of every life of the ones featured in each memorial. Genealogical research is conducted thoroughly as well as communication with family members. We encourage the submission of documents from family members when possible for them. Each memorial contains compilations of such records as well as a collection of photos that document the lives of those memorialized here.

Reich Memorials does not do work for the public and is not a for profit organisation. Memorials are created for a choice few. Our service is highly limited and extremely exclusive.
